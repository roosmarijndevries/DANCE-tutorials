About

    Hi! Our names are Sarah and Roos.
    We're from Amsterdam, The Netherlands and we've met while studying on the University of Amsterdam.
    We both love to dance, so we decided to create something that enables us to share this passion.
    This is when we've started building Learn to DANCE!
    Learn to DANCE! is a unique internet experience that enables our users to learn a variety of 
    dancestyles. We work together with the best YouTube-tutorialist to offer you the best, and 
    only the best, learning-materials. Want to learn how to breakdance like a real b-boy? Or you
    prefer performing a modern dance? You're at the right place! Take a look around, and discover
    the magical world of DANCE!
